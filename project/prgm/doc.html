Drawing a curve

You can choose the type of curve with two switching buttons

A general curve

Use left button on your mouse. The default curve are smooth, to make segments, just double click.

Drawing an ellipse

click to set the first and second point defining the circle (inner to the rectangle formed by the two points)

Aviable buttons:

  • start/stop drawing
  • remove the last point of the curve
  • restart the curve

Drawing lines

Use the settings below the canvas to modify their number and the angle between them, and click on the check button to display them

Draw dots

The dots represents the intersections between the lines and the curve, they are used in the estimation of the curve lenght

There are two ways of drawing dots:

Manually

There are two modes: add and delete, use the switching buttons to select one of them.
Use the right click to add/delete a dot

Automatically

Just click on the "auto" button :)
